Who We Are

Passion, Artistry & Elegance

Helma's Décor, LLC is a small interior decorating company that offers "Elegance at an Affordable Price", a personal touch, and pays attention to every detail!

Located in Fleming Island, we service the Jacksonville area, St. Johns and Clay Counties.

Helma has been an artist all her life — from working in Bridal Designing in Chicago to painting as an artist at Trader Joe's in Florida. Today she is licensed and certified in Interior Decorating, bringing the full breadth of her creative life to every project.

We offer decorating services, guidance, color selection, space planning, and so much more. Designing, Decorating, and Art are in her blood!
