How It Works

Bringing the Vision to Life

Our simple 3-step process takes you from inspiration to a beautifully finished space.

1

Consultation

We start with a conversation. Share your ideas, inspiration, budget, and goals — Helma listens and begins forming your vision.

2

Meeting & Plan

A detailed decorating plan is created — covering colors, furnishings, layout, materials, and all contractor work. You approve every detail.

3

Installation

Helma and her team bring the plan to life. From painting and tile to final accessories — your space is transformed.
